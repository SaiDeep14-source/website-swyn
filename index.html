About us

Led by people who understand the weight of senior decisions.

SWYN was created to help Indian companies access seasoned leadership at the exact moment it matters: during growth, transition, transformation, or a high-stakes mandate.

Founder & CEO

Shaamlie Anggane

Shaamlie brings over 23 years of experience across some of the world's most demanding financial institutions — Nomura, Deutsche Bank, UBS, and Morgan Stanley — where she held senior leadership roles spanning COO, Business Management, and operational strategy across India and global markets.

SWYN was built on a simple idea, growing businesses should have access to experienced leadership without the commitment of a permanent executive hire. The foundation of SWYN is built on the belief that the right expertise, delivered at the right time, can create meaningful business impact. Today, SWYN helps companies engage seasoned CXOs and domain experts who bring practical experience, strategic insight, and measurable impact.
